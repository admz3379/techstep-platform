Payment Terms

1. Payment Options

TechStep Foundation offers two payment options for the SOC Analyst Foundations course:

Full Payment Option

  • One-time payment of $2,849
  • Immediate full course access
  • No future charges
  • Best value option

Monthly Payment Plan

  • $750 down payment (charged today)
  • $375/month for 6 additional months
  • Total of 7 payments: $750 + (6 Ã— $375) = $3,000
  • Automatic billing on the same date each month
  • Course access begins immediately upon down payment

2. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through Stripe, Inc. We accept:

  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Debit cards
  • Apple Pay (on supported devices)
  • Google Pay (on supported devices)

3. Billing and Charges

For monthly payment plans:

  • Your payment method will be charged automatically each month
  • Billing occurs on the same calendar date as your initial payment
  • You will receive email notifications before each charge
  • Failed payments may result in course access suspension

4. Failed Payments and Late Fees

If a monthly payment fails:

  • We will attempt to process payment again within 3 days
  • A $25 late fee will be applied for failed payments
  • Course access may be suspended until payment is received
  • After 7 days, an additional $15 processing fee applies

5. Cancellation and Refunds

Monthly payment plans:

  • You may cancel with 7-day written notice
  • Cancellation prevents future charges but does not refund past payments
  • Course access continues until the end of your paid period
  • No refunds are provided for monthly payments already processed

Full payment option:

  • 7-day money-back guarantee from date of payment
  • After 7 days, all payments are final and non-refundable
  • Refund requests must be submitted to support@techstepfoundation.org

6. Payment Method Updates

You are responsible for keeping your payment information current. To update your payment method:

  • Log into your student dashboard
  • Navigate to "Payment Settings"
  • Update your payment method before the next billing date
  • Contact support if you need assistance

7. Taxes

All prices are in USD and do not include applicable taxes. You are responsible for any taxes that may apply to your purchase based on your location.

8. Currency and Exchange Rates

All payments are processed in US Dollars (USD). If your payment method uses a different currency, your bank or card issuer will handle the conversion at their current exchange rate.

9. Security

Your payment information is protected with:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption
  • PCI DSS compliance
  • Secure payment processing through Stripe
  • No storage of payment details on our servers
